Dropper


Tired of complex cloud solutions and concerned about your privacy? And no solutions is proper? Introducing Dropper! Dropper is the ultimate file-dropping tool hosted within this private home network. With this user-friendly system, share and drop files from devices connected to this home network without compromising your data's privacy.

Get started!
Click on upload files to upload files. To view all the files that are uploaded, click on view files. To switch between light and dark mode, click on the moon icon or the sun icon.
